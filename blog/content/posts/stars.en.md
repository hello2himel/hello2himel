Spotting stars in this city's sky is like finding a fig flower. Despite having five-star and four-star hotels, motels, and resorts, there's no chance to see real 'stars' or breathe freely. Theaters and cineplexes have taken over the infinite star-filled screen of the night sky. Pollution, noise, and tastelessness have turned this city's sky into a strange grey matter.
Tonight the sky is clear. Stars are visible. Good night.
