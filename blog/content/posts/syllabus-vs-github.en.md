I sat down with the syllabus. Let me note which chapters are left — I ended up writing down the entire syllabus.

I can tell I need to sit down and study. But I'm just not getting 'that urge to study.'

Meanwhile, I sat down to automate some shell scripts. Four or five hours of the day went into that. Instead of completing the syllabus, I'm going around resolving GitHub issues.

I'm cursing myself. Then going right back to sit in front of the computer.

Himel, if you don't want to regret your acts, now go, study. You can code later.
