Language is like a flowing river. Ever-moving. Change is the nature of language over time.
If you try to halt the evolution of language in the name of grammar, all you'll get is a pot of muddy water scooped from the river.
Grammar came for language. Language didn't come for grammar. Language remains pure as long as it flows. Let it flow.
