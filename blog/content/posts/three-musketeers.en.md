I am an emotionless, robotic person. But listening to The Three Musketeers, I see this hydrophobic heart getting drenched.
What doesn't this story have! Heroism, patriotism, love, betrayal, deception, foresight, adultery, friendship, sense of duty, forgiveness, unfulfillment — you'll find a beautiful mix of everything.

I don't want to say much more. The book is a must-read. You can also listen to the Sunday Suspense audiobook, as I did. Judge for yourself how much the chess moves of 17th-century imperialist France resemble the 21st-century capitalist rat race.

Thanks to my friend Rupom for suggesting another quality audiobook. The guy sometimes consumes some really quality stuff.
